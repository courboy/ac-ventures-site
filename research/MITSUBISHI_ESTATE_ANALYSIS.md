## Executive Summary

Mitsubishi Estate (MEC) has **46+ active development projects** across Japan, Europe, Asia, and Oceania. Total development pipeline estimated at **¥1+ trillion** (£5.5B+). Key finding: MEC is actively pursuing **capital recycling** — divesting stabilized assets in Australia to reinvest in growth projects.

**Strategic Insight for Evo Lake:** MEC is a "build-to-core" developer that develops, stabilizes, then recycles capital. Best opportunities are:
1. **Stabilized logistics assets** (completed Logicross facilities in Japan)
2. **Australian residential/land lease** (active divestment program)
3. **Partnership on UK logistics** (Bromley project, smaller scale than core focus)

1. **180 George Street** — Sold 30% stake (Aug 2024), first Australia divestment

**Implication:** MEC follows "develop → stabilize → recycle" model. **Stabilized assets are acquisition targets.**
