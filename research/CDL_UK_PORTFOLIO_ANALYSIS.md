## Executive Summary

City Developments Limited (CDL) owns a substantial UK portfolio across **four asset classes**: Commercial Office, Hospitality (M&C Hotels), Build-to-Rent/Residential, and Purpose-Built Student Accommodation (PBSA). Total estimated UK book value: **£1.8-2.2 billion** (excluding hotel operations valued via M&C).

**Key Insight:** CDL has been actively recycling capital since privatizing M&C Hotels in 2019. They've divested multiple non-core assets (South Korea, Britain, US hotels; Ransome's Wharf sold Jan 2025). This signals an openness to structured divestments — ideal for Evo Lake's "embedded investment manager" value proposition.
